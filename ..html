Classroom Rules

  • Be RespectfulNo cursing, harassment, or talking back. Treat others with kindness and avoid rude language.
  • Wait Your TurnNo blurting. Raise your hand and wait your turn to ensure fairness.
  • Be ResponsibleOwn up to mistakes, follow rules, and make good choices.
  • Be KindSupport and encourage classmates. No bullying or judgment.
  • Stay FocusedAvoid distractions to maintain a productive learning environment.
